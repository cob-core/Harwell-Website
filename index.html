About the Game

Establish mining operations, refine raw resources, and manufacture advanced goods. Engage in a dynamic player-driven market, strategically buying low and selling high to accumulate wealth.
The ultimate goal? Acquire enough capital to buy out your opponents and claim total monopoly on the new planet - using any means necessary.

Production Chains

Mine valuable minerals from the Martian crust and feed those resources into your factories to produce complex goods.

Dynamic Economy

Engage in real-time trading with other players using the dynamically changing simulation market system that sets prices based on real-world economics.

Corporate Diplomacy

Fight off the other corporations by making deals with the colony, fight to implement regulations via the Mars Commerce Forum, or sabotage competitors with black market actions.
