About the Game

In Haswell, players embark on an exciting journey to conquer the Martian economy. Starting with humble beginnings, you'll establish mining operations, refine raw resources, and manufacture advanced goods. Engage in a dynamic player-driven market, strategically buying low and selling high to accumulate wealth. The ultimate goal? Acquire enough capital to buy out your opponents and claim total economic supremacy on Mars!

Resource Extraction

Mine valuable minerals and gases from the Martian crust to fuel your industrial empire.

Production Chains

Transform raw resources into complex goods through intricate production lines.

Dynamic Economy

Engage in real-time trading with other players, influencing market prices and securing profits.
